The Scheduling Challenge in NEP 2020

Modern higher education institutions face unprecedented complexity in timetable creation with the implementation of NEP 2020's flexible, multidisciplinary approach.

Manual Timetable Creation is No Longer Sustainable

The National Education Policy 2020 introduces flexible credit-based systems, multidisciplinary courses, and multiple entry-exit options that create scheduling complexities traditional methods can't handle.

  • Managing hundreds of course combinations and student preferences
  • Accommodating faculty availability across departments
  • Optimizing classroom and resource utilization
  • Ensuring compliance with NEP 2020 framework requirements
  • Minimizing scheduling conflicts for students and faculty
